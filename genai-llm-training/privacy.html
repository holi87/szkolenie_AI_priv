Privacy

This training is fully static and sends none of your data to a server — there is no backend, no analytics, no cookies and no third-party tools. Everything runs in your browser.

What we store locally in your browser

In localStorage (on your device) we keep only what is needed for the training to work and remember your progress:

  • the chosen path, module status and time, and your last location,
  • quiz and final-test results, plus completion-certificate data,
  • the selected colour theme (light/dark) and the selected interface language,
  • keys: genai-training:progress:<path>, genai-training:cursor, genai-training:theme and genai-training:lang.

The certificate alias is NOT saved. We keep it only in this session's memory — it disappears when you refresh or close the tab. Please also use an alias, not your real name.

Your data never leaves your device

We do not transmit anything. The files you can download (JSON/CSV with results) and optionally send to a coordinator do not contain your alias — they contain only anonymous identifiers (a completion ID without the alias), results and times; the completion date is truncated to the day so it does not become an identifier.

How to delete your data

At any time use the “Start over” button in the app (clears all saved progress for this browser) or clear the site data in your browser settings.

Cookies and legal basis

We use no cookies and no tracking, so there is no need for a cookie consent banner. Storing your progress locally is strictly necessary for the service you request yourself (completing the training and issuing a certificate) — this is the “strictly necessary” exception under Art. 5(3) of the ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC), so we do not ask for consent. Treat this page as the information notice under Art. 13 GDPR.
